How to tie shoe laces

In this website, I will tell you how to tie your shoes if you don't know how. If we know how to tie our shoes, we don't have to walk with our shoes untied. To tie your shoes, we just need 2 materials.

Materials needed:

  • Shoe
  • Shoelaces

    • Step 1

    We should first make an "X" with shoelaces.

    Step 2

    Hold the end of under lace and put it in an "X" made by the laces, inside it.

    Step 3

    We pull it hard so that the laces won't get loose.

    Step 4

    We hold the laces and fold about 1/3 of it.

    Step 5

    Hold those folded laces and make an "X" again.

    Step 6

    Hold the end of the under lace and put it in "X" as we did in Step 2.

    Step 7

    Don't pull it hard yet. We should find a part where the lace won't come out even if you pull.

    Step 8

    Once you find that part for both sides, then pull it as hard as possible to hold it tight.

    End

    If you correctly followed all these steps, you will have tightly tied shoelaces.

    Then it's finished!
